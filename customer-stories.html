Customer Stories 2

Laser cutting is the complete removal and separation of material from the top surface to the bottom surface along a designated path. Due to its high melt temperature, and inherent purity, Teflon® ablates cleanly from the bulk material. The edges that result from laser cutting Teflon® are smooth and free from discoloration sometimes associated with thermal processes. Laser cutting Teflon® will produce a fine powder of PTFE particles, which is easily cleaned with a dry cloth. The capability shown can be extended to nearly any shape, even complex and closely-spaced cuts.
