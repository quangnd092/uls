The XLS10MWH MultiWave Hybrid™ is the first and only laser system to combine multiple laser wavelengths and power into a single coaxial beam. The XLS10 MultiWave Hybrid accommodates a fixed fiber laser source and two different CO2 laser wavelength sources that can be independently or simultaneously controlled for a virtually infinite number of wavelength and power combinations. As a result the MultiWave Hybrid can process any material, even difficult multi-layered materials with conductive and non-conductive layers.
