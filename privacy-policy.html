Privacy Policy

Universal Laser Privacy Policy Last Updated July 1, 2010

At Universal Laser Systems, Inc. (“Universal Laser”) your trust and satisfaction is of the utmost importance. Because we respect your privacy, we have instituted this Privacy Policy to ensure that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. We reserve the right to change the provisions of this Privacy Policy at any time. We will alert you that changes have been made by indicating on the Privacy Policy the date it was last updated. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy from time to time to make sure that you understand how any personal information you provide will be used.

What this Privacy Policy Covers

This Privacy Policy covers how Universal Laser treats personal information that it collects and receives with respect to your use of the website located at www.ulsinc.com (the “Website’) and any services accessible through the Website (the “Services”). Personal information is information about you that is personally identifiable such as your name, address, email address, phone number or other information about you when it is directly linked with your personally identifiable information. Personal information does not include “aggregate” information, which is data we collect about the use of the Website or about a group or category of products, services or users, from which individual identities or other personal information is not attached or has been removed. This Privacy Policy does not apply to aggregate information. It also does not apply to any companies or websites that Universal Laser does not own or control, or to people that Universal Laser does not employ or manage.

Information We Collect

You may choose to provide your personal information (such as your name, address, telephone number and email address) to Universal Laser through the Website. Here are some of the ways you may provide personal information, information and the types of information you may submit.

Personal Information

Universal Laser may collect personal information in a number of ways. We may collect certain information you voluntarily provide to us. For example, we may collect your name, user name, email address, telephone number, other contact and demographic information when you register, set up an account, fill out a form, submit a comment or contact us by email or other means. Also, we occasionally may ask you to complete online surveys about your activities, attitudes and interests with our products. These surveys help us serve you better and improve the usefulness of our Website. In conducting these surveys we may ask you for your name and email address. Personal information may also be collected if you participate in any interactive forums or features on the Website or through the Services. Universal Laser may combine information about you that we have with information we obtain from business partners or other companies. When you use the Website, some information is also automatically collected, such as your Internet Protocol (IP) address, your operating system, the browser type, the address of a referring website, and your activity on the Website. We treat this information as personally information only if we combine it with or link it to any of the identifying information mentioned above. Otherwise, it is used in the aggregate only and not considered your personal information.

Cookies and Web Beacons

We may automatically collect certain information through the use of “cookies” or “web beacons.”

We use cookies in conjunction with our Website and advertisements. A cookie typically consists of a small data file generated by a web page server and stored on your computer's hard drive at the request of a website to enable the site to recognize users who have previously visited them and retain certain information such as customary preferences and history. Cookies, by themselves, are not able to extract any personal information from you. Unless you specifically tell us, we will not know who you are even though we may assign your computer a cookie. You can control the creation of cookies through your web browser. However, you may not be able to use certain functions and features of a website if you disable cookies. We use the information from cookies to evaluate and improve our Websites and advertisements. If we link cookies to any personally identifying information, we will treat this information as personal information.

We may also use web beacons in connection with our Website and advertisements. Web beacons (also known as clear gifs) are small, invisible, graphic imagines that may be used on the Website or in emails relating to the Website to collect certain information and monitor user activity on the Website.

We may also use third parties to serve ads on the Website. These third parties may place cookies, web beacons or other devices on your computer to collect non-personal information, and information provided by these devices may be used, among other things, to analyze data, to determine the popularity of certain content, to deliver advertising targeted to your interests and to better understand the usage and visitation of our Website and the other sites tracked by these third parties. This policy does not apply to, and we are not responsible for, cookies or web beacons in third party ads, and we encourage you to check the privacy policies of advertisers and/or ad services to learn about their use of cookies, web beacons, and other technology.

Use of Personal Information

Universal Laser may use your personal information for the following purposes: to customize the advertising and content you see, to fulfill your requests for products and services, to improve the Website and our Services, to contact you, to conduct research and provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients, to provide you with information you request, to inform you about events, promotions, products, services or other information that we think may interest you, to facilitate your use of, and our administration and operation of the Website and Services, to analyze data and trends, and for the purpose for you provided the information.

Universal Laser does not sell or otherwise disclose personal information, except as described in this Privacy Policy, as revised from time to time. Universal Laser may share information provided to vendors, consultants, affiliates and other services providers who are engaged by or working with us in connection with the operation of the Website or the services and who need access to such information to carry out their work for Universal Laser. These service providers are contractually restricted from using or disclosing the information except as necessary to perform services on our behalf or to comply with legal requirements. However, Universal Laser is not responsible for the actions of service providers or other third parties, nor are we responsible for any additional information you provide directly to these service providers or other third parties, and Universal Laser encourages you to become familiar with their practices before disclosing information directly to them. If you send us a notification of claimed infringement under the Digital Millennium Copyright Act, any personal information in your notification may be shared with the person or entity who you claim is infringing your copyrighted material. Information about Universal Laser’s users, including personal information, may also be disclosed or transferred as part of, or during negotiations of, any merger, sale of company assets, financing or acquisition or in any other similar situation.

In addition, Universal Laser may disclose information about you: (i) when you provide your consent to do so; (ii) if Universal Laser is required to do so by law or legal process; (iii) to law enforcement authorities or other government officials; or (iv) when Universal Laser believes disclosure is necessary or appropriate to prevent physical harm or financial loss or in connection with an investigation of suspected or actual illegal activity. Universal Laser may transfer the personal information we collect on this site to other countries where it does business, but solely for the purposes described above. When Universal Laser transfers your information to other countries, we protect that information as described in this Privacy Policy, unless otherwise required by applicable laws.

You understand that when you use the Website and Services, information you post in connection with your participation in any interactive features, as well as information you share with individuals, may be available to other users and in some cases, may be publicly available. Universal Laser recommends that you exercise caution about giving our your personal information to others or sharing personal information in public or private websites. We are not responsible for the actions of any third parties with whom you share your personal information.

As part of this advertising, third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your past visits to Universal Laser's website.
