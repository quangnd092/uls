XLS Platform

The XLS10MWH MultiWave Hybrid™ is the first and only laser system to combine multiple laser wavelengths and power into a single coaxial beam.

ILS Platform

The ILS12.75 is our largest and most versatile platform. Engineered specifically for manufacturing environments, it can handle applications ranging from prototyping to automated manufacturing.

PLS Platform

The PLS6MW Multi-Wavelength Laser Platform uses multiple laser wavelengths to process the broadest possible spectrum of materials.

VLS Platform

The largest in the VLS platform line, the VLS6.60 has a wide and deep engraving area that can accommodate a variety of materials.
